ABOUT THE CLUB







TINO INVESTORS
MEETING ROOM: ST. JOHN'S ROOM, ROOM 913, WEDNESDAYS AT LUNCH
WELCOME TO THE WORLD OF INVESTING! YOU PROBABLY HAVE SOME
QUESTIONS LIKE WHAT WILL BE TAUGHT AT THE MEETINGS, WHAT OUR
OBJECTIVES ARE FOR MEMBERS, AND HOW EXACTLY YOU WILL LEARN TO
MAKE MONEY.

A QUICK OVERVIEW OF HOW THE CLUB WORKS:

1. THE FIRST FEW WEEKS WILL CONSIST OF TEACHING PEOPLE WHAT
STOCKS ARE AND HOW TO BE SUCCESSFUL AT TRADING THEM (I.E.
TRADING RULES, MARKET TRENDS, INVESTING STYLES, ETC.).
NO PRIOR EXPERIENCE NEEDED.
WE WILL TEACH FROM THE BOTTOM UP.

2. EACH WEEK WILL HAVE SOME TYPE OF TRADING THEME. FOR EXAMPLE,
ONE WEEK COULD BE TRADING COMMODITIES SUCH AS GOLD/OIL, NEXT
WEEK FOCUSES TRADING FOR MEDICINE STARTUPS, THEN THE FOLLOWING
WEEK WOULD BE CENTERED AROUND ELECTRONIC MARKETS, ETC.

3. YOU ARE NOT REQUIRED TO USE YOUR OWN MONEY. IT IS PERFECTLY
FINE TO USE A VIRTUAL ACCOUNT UNTIL YOU FEEL CONFIDENT WITH
YOUR DECISIONS AS AN INVESTOR.

4. RESOURCES SUCH AS IBD, NASDAQ, WALL STREET JOURNAL, AND PRETTY
MUCH ANYTHING THAT IS RELEVANT TO THE CURRENT MARKET WILL BE IN
USE TO HELP THE CLUB AND INDIVIDUALS DECIDE ON WHAT THEY WANT
TO TRADE.

5. THROUGHOUT THE YEAR WE WILL HOLD MANY MINI-COMPETITIONS
AND ONE LARGE COMPETITION AT THE END OF THE YEAR IN WHICH A
VARIETY OF PRIZES WILL BE UP FOR GRABS.

HOPE THIS HELPS CLARIFY HOW THE CLUB OPERATES.
